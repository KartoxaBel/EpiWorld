EpiWorld — is an app that helps address cognitive impairments in children with epilepsy







Children with epilepsy and antiepileptic therapy often face challenges in mastering educational programs due to cognitive impairments.


At the same time, parents and caregivers of these children lack sufficient information on how to help their specific child. An individualized approach to epilepsy treatment is crucial but remains largely inaccessible in today's world.

EpiWorld automates personalized therapy selection, informs caregivers about tailored solutions, and simulates techniques for cognitive rehabilitation therapy.
